The Power of the Base

In the formula Baseⁿ, even a small increase in the base multiplier leads to an enormous divergence in the final outcome. This is why tripling reaches targets significantly faster than doubling.

Base Step 5 Step 10
2 (Double) 32 1,024
3 (Triple) 243 59,049
4 (Quadruple) 1,024 1,048,576
